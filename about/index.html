Features

Works offline

Use nimbo offline. All data is locally stored in IndexedDB

Instant search

Search and navigate to all of your non-archived boards and cards instantly

Light/dark theme

Switch between light and dark theme

Control your data

Import/export your data between browsers. Supports importing from Trello.

Archive and star boards

Archive boards to hide from Zen mode and search palette.

Card details

Add a checklist, description, due date, label and priority to your cards.

Markdown support

Add a description to your cards using Markdown

Sync data between tabs

Changes are synced between your tabs

Zen mode

Use filters to get a simple overview of your next tasks with time tracking
